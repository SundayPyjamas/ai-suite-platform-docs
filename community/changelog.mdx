- **API Status Page**: Check our [status page](https://status.sundaypyjamas.com) for incidents and maintenance

### Migration Guides
When upgrading between major versions, always check our migration guides:
- [Migrating from v1.0 to v1.1](/guides/migration/v1.0-to-v1.1)
- [Migrating from v1.1 to v1.2](/guides/migration/v1.1-to-v1.2)

### Breaking Changes Policy
- **Major Version Changes**: May include breaking changes (e.g., v1.x to v2.x)
- **Minor Version Changes**: Backward compatible (e.g., v1.1 to v1.2)
- **Patch Version Changes**: Bug fixes only (e.g., v1.1.0 to v1.1.1)

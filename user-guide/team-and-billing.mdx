## Inviting your team

A workspace can be shared with others, so your whole team uses the same chats, apps, and agents.

<Steps>
<Step title="Open Settings → Team (or Members)">
  Click **Settings** in the left menu, then find **Team** or **Members**.

  <Frame caption="The Team area, where you invite and manage members.">
    <img src="/images/user-guide/team-members.png" alt="A team members list with an invite button" />
  </Frame>
</Step>
<Step title="Click Invite">
  Enter your teammate's email address.
</Step>
<Step title="Choose their role">
  Roles decide what someone can do — for example, a member who can use everything, or an admin who can also manage settings and billing. Pick the right level.
</Step>
<Step title="Send the invite">
  They'll get an email with a link to join your workspace. Once they accept, they're in.
</Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
Give people the **least access they need** to do their job. You can always upgrade someone's role later.
</Tip>

## Understanding credits and usage

Using AI costs a small amount of computing each time — measured in **credits** (or **tokens**). Don't overthink it:

> Every message, image, or report uses a little bit of your credit balance — like using a little data on a phone plan.

- **Short, simple tasks** use fewer credits.
- **Long tasks and smarter models** use more.
- You can **see your usage** any time so there are no surprises.

## Checking your usage

<Steps>
<Step title="Open Settings → Usage (or Credits & Usage)">
  Click **Settings**, then **Usage** or **Billing**.

  <Frame caption="The usage screen shows how much you've used and how much is left.">
    <img src="/images/user-guide/team-usage.png" alt="A usage dashboard showing credits used and remaining" />
  </Frame>
</Step>
<Step title="Read your balance">
  You'll see how much you've used and how much remains. Some plans show a chart over time.
</Step>
<Step title="Top up or upgrade if needed">
  Running low? You can add credits or move to a bigger plan from the **Billing** screen.
</Step>
</Steps>

## Keeping costs sensible

- **Use faster models** for everyday tasks — they cost less.
- **Be concise** in your prompts (see [Writing good prompts](/user-guide/writing-prompts)).
- **Reuse apps and agents** instead of re-explaining every time.
- **Check usage** now and then so nothing surprises you.

<Note>
Only workspace admins can change billing or the plan. If you can't see billing options, ask your workspace admin.
</Note>

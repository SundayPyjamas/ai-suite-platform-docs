## What is Chat?

**Chat** is the simplest and most useful part of AI Suite. You type a message, the AI reads it, and it types an answer back — just like texting a knowledgeable friend. You can ask it to write, explain, summarize, translate, plan, or brainstorm.

Let's do it together.

## Step by step

<Steps>
<Step title="Open Chat">
  Click **Chat** in the menu on the left. You'll see an empty box at the bottom, waiting for your message.

  <Frame caption="An empty chat, ready for your first message. The typing box is at the bottom.">
    <img src="/images/user-guide/first-chat-empty.png" alt="Empty chat screen with a message box at the bottom" />
  </Frame>
</Step>

<Step title="Type a message">
  Click in the box and type something simple. Try copying this:

  > *Write a friendly thank-you email to a customer named Sam who just bought our coffee subscription.*

  <Tip>
  The clearer you are, the better the answer. Notice we said **who** (a customer named Sam) and **what** (a thank-you email about a coffee subscription). More on this in [Writing good prompts](/user-guide/writing-prompts).
  </Tip>
</Step>

<Step title="Send it">
  Press **Enter**, or click the send button (the little arrow). The AI will start typing its answer within a second or two.

  <Frame caption="Your message appears, and the AI's answer streams in below it.">
    <img src="/images/user-guide/first-chat-answer.png" alt="Chat showing the user's message and the AI's written reply" />
  </Frame>
</Step>

<Step title="Read the answer">
  The AI writes its reply right on the screen. You can read it, copy it, and use it anywhere.
</Step>

<Step title="Ask a follow-up">
  You don't have to start over. Just keep typing in the same chat. For example:

  > *Make it a bit shorter and add a 10% discount code: WELCOME10.*

  The AI remembers what you were talking about and updates its answer. This back-and-forth is what makes Chat powerful.
</Step>
</Steps>

## Handy things you can do with an answer

- **Copy it** — hover over the answer to find the copy button, then paste it into your email or document.
- **Ask for changes** — "make it shorter", "make it more formal", "add bullet points".
- **Start fresh** — click **New chat** (usually a "+" near the top) when you want to change the subject. This keeps topics tidy.

<Note>
Every separate conversation is saved in your chat history on the left, so you can come back to it later. Starting a **New chat** for a new topic keeps things organized.
</Note>

## Common questions

<AccordionGroup>
<Accordion title="Did I break anything? Can I make a mistake?">
  No. You can't break anything by chatting. If an answer isn't what you wanted, just ask again in different words. Experiment freely.
</Accordion>
<Accordion title="The answer looks wrong. What do I do?">
  AI can occasionally be confident but incorrect. For anything important (facts, numbers, legal or medical topics), double-check it. You can also reply *"Are you sure? Please double-check."*
</Accordion>
<Accordion title="Can I use a different AI for a smarter answer?">
  Yes. You can switch the AI "model" — see [Choosing a model](/user-guide/choosing-models).
</Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

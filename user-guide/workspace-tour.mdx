## The big picture

When you log in, the screen has three main parts. Once you know these three, you'll never feel lost.

<Frame caption="The workspace: (1) the menu on the left, (2) the top bar, (3) the big work area in the middle.">
  <img src="/images/user-guide/workspace-tour-overview.png" alt="Full workspace with the sidebar, top bar, and main area labelled" />
</Frame>

<Steps>
<Step title="The menu on the left (the sidebar)">
  This is how you move around. Each item opens a different tool. You'll use this all the time.
</Step>
<Step title="The top bar">
  Shows where you are right now, and holds your profile, notifications, and workspace switcher on the right.
</Step>
<Step title="The main area in the middle">
  This is where the actual work happens — your chat, your images, your settings, and so on.
</Step>
</Steps>

## What's in the left menu?

Here's what each item does, in plain words. Don't worry about memorizing this — just know it's here.

| Menu item | What it's for |
|---|---|
| **Dashboard** | Your home screen. A quick overview and shortcuts. |
| **Chat** | Talk to the AI. Ask questions, write things, brainstorm. |
| **Images** | Make pictures by describing them. |
| **Reports** | Turn your information into clean written summaries. |
| **Apps** | Build and manage your own mini AI helpers. |
| **Agents** | Build AI helpers that can use tools and do multi-step tasks. |
| **Storage** | Your files and uploaded documents live here. |
| **Integrations** | Connect other tools you use (like Slack or a calendar). |
| **Settings** | Your account, your team, billing, and API keys. |

<Note>
On a phone, the menu moves to the **bottom** of the screen and shows the most-used items: Dashboard, Apps, Agents, and Settings. Tap them the same way.
</Note>

## The top-right corner

<Frame caption="Top-right: switch workspaces, check notifications, and open your profile.">
  <img src="/images/user-guide/workspace-tour-topbar.png" alt="Top bar right-hand side with workspace switcher, notifications bell, and profile menu" />
</Frame>

- **Workspace switcher** — if you belong to more than one workspace, switch between them here.
- **Notifications (the bell)** — updates and alerts show up here.
- **Your profile** — click your picture or initials to reach account settings and to log out.

## A handy shortcut

Press **Ctrl + K** (or **Cmd + K** on a Mac) anywhere to open a search box. Start typing where you want to go — like "chat" or "settings" — and jump straight there. It's the fastest way to move around once you're comfortable.

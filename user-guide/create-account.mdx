## What is a "workspace"?

A **workspace** is your own private area inside AI Suite. It holds your chats, your files, your apps, and your settings. If you work with a team, you can all share one workspace. Think of it like your desk at work — your stuff lives there, and you decide who else can sit at it.

## Sign up (first time only)

<Steps>
<Step title="Open the sign-up page">
  Go to your AI Suite web address (for example, **suite.sundaypyjamas.com**). You'll see a box asking you to sign in or sign up.

  <Frame caption="The login screen. Click the 'Sign up' tab to create a new account.">
    <img src="/images/user-guide/create-account-login.png" alt="AI Suite login screen with Sign in and Sign up tabs" />
  </Frame>
</Step>
<Step title="Click 'Sign up'">
  Switch to the **Sign up** tab at the top of the box.
</Step>
<Step title="Enter your email and a password">
  Use an email you can check right now. Pick a password you'll remember (or save it in your password manager).
</Step>
<Step title="Confirm your email">
  Check your inbox for a message from AI Suite and click the confirmation link. This proves the email is really yours.

  <Note>
  No email after a minute? Check your spam or junk folder, then try again.
  </Note>
</Step>
</Steps>

## Log in (every time after that)

<Steps>
<Step title="Enter your email and password">
  On the **Sign in** tab, type the email and password you used to sign up, then click **Sign in with email**.

  <Frame caption="Type your email and password, then click 'Sign in with email'.">
    <img src="/images/user-guide/create-account-signin.png" alt="Sign in form with email and password fields" />
  </Frame>
</Step>
<Step title="Land in your workspace">
  You're in! You'll see your **Dashboard** — the home screen of your workspace. The next page gives you a full tour.
</Step>
</Steps>

## Forgot your password?

No problem — this happens to everyone.

<Steps>
<Step title="Click 'Forgot password?'">
  It's the small link next to the password box.
</Step>
<Step title="Enter your email">
  We'll send you a reset link.
</Step>
<Step title="Set a new password">
  Open the link and choose a new password. Then log in as usual.
</Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
Keep your password private. AI Suite staff will **never** ask you for it. If you get an email asking for your password, don't reply — it's not from us.
</Warning>

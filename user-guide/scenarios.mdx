## Marketer: turn notes into a campaign brief

**Goal:** Turn messy brainstorming notes into a clean campaign brief.

<Steps>
<Step title="Open Chat">
  Go to [Chat](/user-guide/first-chat).
</Step>
<Step title="Paste your notes and ask">
  > *Turn these rough notes into a one-page campaign brief with sections for Goal, Audience, Key Message, and 3 Content Ideas: [paste notes]*
</Step>
<Step title="Refine">
  Ask for tweaks: *"Make the key message punchier"* or *"Add a catchy campaign name."*
</Step>
<Step title="Save it as a Report">
  Want a polished, shareable version? Recreate it as a [Report](/user-guide/reports) and [share the link](/user-guide/sharing).
</Step>
</Steps>

## Support lead: build a help-desk assistant

**Goal:** An assistant that answers customer questions in your brand's voice, from your real help docs.

<Steps>
<Step title="Create an app or assistant">
  Start with [Build an app](/user-guide/build-an-app) or an [Assistant](/user-guide/assistants).
</Step>
<Step title="Write clear instructions">
  > *You are our friendly support helper. Answer only from the uploaded help docs. Keep replies short and warm. If unsure, offer to connect the customer to a human.*
</Step>
<Step title="Upload your help docs">
  Add your FAQ and policies as [knowledge](/user-guide/knowledge) so answers are accurate.
</Step>
<Step title="Test with real questions">
  Try the awkward ones your team actually gets. Adjust the instructions until it's right.
</Step>
<Step title="Share it with the team">
  [Share the link](/user-guide/sharing) so everyone can use it.
</Step>
</Steps>

## Analyst: ask questions of a document

**Goal:** Get answers out of a long report or contract without reading every page.

<Steps>
<Step title="Upload the document">
  Add your file as [knowledge](/user-guide/knowledge).
</Step>
<Step title="Ask specific questions">
  > *What are the payment terms?* · *List every deadline mentioned.* · *Summarize the risks in 5 bullets.*
</Step>
<Step title="Check the source">
  Ask *"Which section is that from?"* to verify before you rely on it.
</Step>
</Steps>

## Small business owner: weekly summary report

**Goal:** A tidy weekly recap you can send to your team or keep for records.

<Steps>
<Step title="Gather the week's info">
  Paste in sales numbers, customer notes, or highlights.
</Step>
<Step title="Create a Report">
  In [Reports](/user-guide/reports): *"Create a one-page weekly summary with Wins, Problems, and Next Week's Focus."*
</Step>
<Step title="Make it repeatable">
  Turn it into an [app](/user-guide/build-an-app) so next week you just paste new numbers and go.
</Step>
</Steps>

## Teacher: make a simple lesson

**Goal:** Turn a topic into a kid-friendly explanation and a few questions.

<Steps>
<Step title="Ask in Chat">
  > *Explain the water cycle for 8-year-olds in 5 short sentences, then give me 3 easy quiz questions with answers.*
</Step>
<Step title="Adjust the level">
  *"Make it simpler"* or *"Add a fun example."*
</Step>
<Step title="Make a picture">
  Use [Images](/user-guide/images): *"A simple, colorful diagram of the water cycle for kids."*
</Step>
</Steps>

## Anyone: tidy up your writing

**Goal:** Make any message clearer and friendlier before you send it.

<Steps>
<Step title="Paste your draft in Chat">
  > *Rewrite this to be clear, friendly, and under 80 words: [paste your message]*
</Step>
<Step title="Ask for options">
  *"Give me 3 versions: formal, casual, and very short."* Pick the one you like.
</Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
Notice the pattern in every scenario: **describe what you want clearly, give the AI your material, then refine.** That's 90% of using AI Suite well.
</Tip>

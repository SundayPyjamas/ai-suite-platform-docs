## What is AI Suite?

AI Suite is a website where you can **talk to a smart AI assistant**, ask it to write things, make pictures, read your documents, and even build your own little AI helpers — all without writing any code.

Think of it like having a very fast, very patient helper who is good at reading, writing, and organizing. You type what you want in plain English, and it helps you get it done.

<Note>
**You do not need to be technical to use this guide.** Every step has pictures and plain-language explanations. Read the pages in order the first time — each one builds on the last.
</Note>

## The 3-minute version

If you only read one thing, read this:

<Steps>
<Step title="Log in">
  Go to your workspace and sign in. See [Create your account](/user-guide/create-account).
</Step>
<Step title="Open Chat">
  Click **Chat** in the menu on the left.
</Step>
<Step title="Type what you want">
  For example: *"Write a friendly thank-you email to a customer named Sam."*
</Step>
<Step title="Press Enter and read the answer">
  That's it — you just used AI. Everything else in this guide builds on this one skill.
</Step>
</Steps>
